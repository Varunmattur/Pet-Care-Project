# Pet Care EHR – Electronic Health Records System

The **Pet Care EHR** project is designed to provide a digital solution for managing veterinary health records. It enables pet owners and veterinary clinics to store, access, and update medical information in a structured and secure manner. This repository demonstrates applied knowledge in healthcare systems, database management, and user-centric application design.

---

## 📖 Overview

Managing pet health records can be challenging when information is scattered across paper files or multiple systems. This project addresses that problem by offering a centralized **Electronic Health Record (EHR)** system tailored for pets. It ensures that vaccination history, medical treatments, prescriptions, and diagnostic reports are easily accessible and well-organized.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- **[Centralized Records](ca://s?q=Explain_centralized_records_in_pet_care)** – Maintain all pet medical data in one place.  
- **[Accessibility](ca://s?q=Explain_accessibility_in_pet_care_systems)** – Allow owners and vets to access records anytime, anywhere.  
- **[Data Security](ca://s?q=Explain_data_security_in_pet_care_EHR)** – Protect sensitive health information with secure storage.  
- **[Efficiency](ca://s?q=Explain_efficiency_in_pet_care_EHR)** – Reduce paperwork and streamline veterinary workflows.  

---

## 📂 Features

- **Pet Profiles** – Store details such as name, breed, age, and owner information.  
- **Medical History** – Track vaccinations, treatments, allergies, and surgeries.  
- **Prescription Management** – Record medications and dosage instructions.  
- **Reports & Diagnostics** – Upload lab results and diagnostic images.  
- **User Roles** – Different access levels for veterinarians, staff, and pet owners.
